ABOUT US
MISSION_STATEMENT.TXT

The Rutgers Nuclear & Fusion Society is a student-run organization dedicated to exploring the science, engineering, and policy of nuclear energy and fusion research.

We bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application through hands-on projects, guest lectures, and collaborative research initiatives.

Whether you're a physicist, engineer, policy wonk, or just curious about nuclear physics and fusion, there's a place for you here.

CORE_DIRECTIVES.SYS
  • Advance student understanding of nuclear physics and fusion science
  • Connect members with industry professionals and research labs
  • Engage the Rutgers community on energy policy and nuclear literacy
  • Support members pursuing careers in nuclear engineering and research
  • Collaborate with national labs, startups, and academic institutions
